About Me

About Me

Hi, I'm Amanda, a computer science major and design thinking minor undergraduate with a great interest in UX/UI design. I'm passionate about designing smart digital experiences that combine usefulness with visual appeal. My studies enable me to approach projects from both technical and artistic standpoints, whether I'm creating responsive websites, designing interfaces, or researching new ways to increase accessibility and usability.

I enjoy collaborating on projects that require me to think critically about user demands and push the limits of what technology and design can do together. I believe that good design is inclusive, thoughtful, and effective, and I am always ready to learn new tools and strategies to improve my work.
